About

Adam Harris

BBC Introducing  ·  Leicester, UK

Adam Harris is a UK pop artist blending cinematic production with emotionally driven songwriting. Drawing inspiration from ZAYN and Arjun Artist, his sound is slow, rhythmic, and unmistakably his own.

His debut single Wavelength earned a play on BBC Introducing — the UK's most respected launchpad for emerging artists. He explores themes of resilience, love, and personal evolution with atmospheric production that lingers long after the song ends.

Independent, intentional, and just getting started.
