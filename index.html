About

Adam Harris

BBC Introducing  ·  Leicester, UK

Adam Harris is a UK pop artist blending cinematic production with emotionally driven songwriting. His music explores themes of resilience, love, and personal evolution while combining modern pop melodies with atmospheric production.

Raised in Leicester and drawing inspiration from ZAYN and MNEK, Adam has carved out a sound that is slow, rhythmic, and unmistakably his own. His debut single Wavelength earned a play on BBC Introducing — the UK's most respected launchpad for emerging talent.

As an independent artist, he focuses on building a body of work that connects emotionally while maintaining strong visual storytelling. The music is just getting started.
